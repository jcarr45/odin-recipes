Cannoli Dip

Description

Ricotta. Like a true cannoli, this dip has a creamy ricotta base. Cream cheese. Helps firm up the texture so that this dip is dippable. Powdered sugar and vanilla. For adding sweetness and flavor to the ricotta and cream cheese. Mini chocolate chips. For a crunchy texture and a touch of chocolate. Waffle cones. To mimic the shell of a cannoli, these are broken up and used for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ricotta
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup mini chocolate chips
  • waffle cones, broken into dippable pieces
