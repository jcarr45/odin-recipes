Cannoli Dip

Description

Ricotta. Like a true cannoli, this dip has a creamy ricotta base. Cream cheese. Helps firm up the texture so that this dip is dippable. Powdered sugar and vanilla. For adding sweetness and flavor to the ricotta and cream cheese. Mini chocolate chips. For a crunchy texture and a touch of chocolate. Waffle cones. To mimic the shell of a cannoli, these are broken up and used for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ricotta
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup mini chocolate chips
  • waffle cones, broken into dippable pieces

Steps

  1. Using a stand mixer (or a hand mixer + large bowl), beat ricotta, cream cheese, and powdered sugar on medium-high speed until smooth, about 3-5 minutes. Add in vanilla extract and whip for another 30 seconds.
  2. Taste test the dip. If youâ€™d like it sweeter, add more powdered sugar, about 2 tablespoons at a time. You can safely add up to another 1/2 cup of powdered sugar (8 tablespoons) without negatively impacting consistency.
  3. Fold in mini chocolate chips, reserving some to sprinkle on top for decoration, if desired.
  4. Serve dip immediately with broken waffle cones for dipping.
